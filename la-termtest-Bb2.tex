\begin{equation}
f(x)\approx{}E+\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
A&B \\
C&D
\end{array}\right]\left[
\begin{array}{c}
x-M \\
y-N
\end{array}\right]
\end{equation}
Specify the numbers $A,B,C,D,E,M,N$ in your solution.
