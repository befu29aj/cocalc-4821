\textbf{Term Test Bb version 1}

\aufgabe{5} Consider the following function:
\begin{equation}
f\left(\left[
\begin{array}{c}
x \\
y
\end{array}\left]\right)=\left[
\begin{array}{c}
x^{2}+x\sin(x+y) \\
\sin{}x\cos(x+y)
\end{array}\right]
\end{equation}
Linearize the function around $x=1,y=2$ so it looks as follows,
\begin{equation}
f(x)\approx{}E+\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
A&B \\
C&D
\end{array}\right]\left[
\begin{array}{c}
x-M \\
x-N
\end{array}\right]
\end{equation}
Specify the numbers $A,B,C,D,E,M,N$ in your solution.
